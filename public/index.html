Help us take this to the next level

We want to continue developing this project, since we think it is relevant and cool. But we also have our daily lives to manage :)

The Umeboshi Fireteam is not a company, just a group of friends. Free thinking developers from Brazil that have other commitments of their own. Our group is a mix of freelancers, student, unemployed and employed people, and while we would love to be able/sponsored to focus deeply into this project, for now we are working on it on our spare time and coordinating the tasks over the mailing list. Which means that the help from the community is vital.

So please, join our group to be part of the conversation, fill this form to leave a comment and be notified of important updates and releases, or simply follow the git repository to watch the updates as they happen.
